Everyone comes to the crossroads of their life and our mission is to help the students to choose the right path when they are stuck in the crossroads of their career. Crossroads has crossed twenty thousand plus subscribers and ten thousand plus watch hours on YouTube.

Founded in 2019 by Nikhil Kilivayil,He is a Software Engineer turned Entrepreneur who has 9+ years of work experience in the software industry and is now successfully running a Software Consultancy business in Calicut. His passion for teaching had led him to start an educational institution where he teaches how to learn for yourself and build a good career in the tech industry.
